Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I order food?

Go to the "Order Food" section from the main menu and select your desired items.

2. Can I track my order?

Yes, use the "View Orders" option to see the status of your current orders.

3. How can I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.
